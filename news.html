False Information on the Internet

The Reality and How to Better Educate Yourself

News Sources:

News digital platforms are typically used through outlets like Television, online articles, and radio/podcasts. In the United State, the biggest issue between news channels is that they are starting to become more ideological and have politically leaning agendas. The most often viewed news sources when it comes to government and politics are Fox News and CNN. Those who have conservative political values tend to watch Fox News, while those with more liberal political values seem to watch CNN. While this is not to say that if you lean more towards one side of the political spectrum you cannot watch both or even neither, this is just what research has examined.

In current times, many biased news sources are starting to use targeted vocabulary and twist facts into disinformation to devalue the opposite politically leaning sources. This is harmful as it’s causing news resources, that are supposed to be reliable, to become another platform to spread false information; making people feel overwhelmed and form trust issues with their news channel. However, this also has the opposite effect on some viewers where because they feel that the new source should be reliable, they take what is said to be completely factual and do not look into it further.

All news sources have two main segments of news. One category segment is for news that covers events that are presently relevant and have been out long enough for all the evidence to be factual. This way everything should be up-to-date with facts. The other category is breaking news. This is where the events are happening in real-time. During these events, not all the information has been found and fact-checked for accuracy. It is common that breaking news will get some information wrong during the event and later submit corrections. This is where politically opposite news sources use their targeted vocabulary and options the most. In this step, a lot of misinformation is formed as many people do not know for sure what is truly accurate and what is exaggerated to fit an agenda.
